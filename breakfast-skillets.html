Poached Egg Melt

Amount:

  • 1 Serving

Time:

  • 10 Minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 - large egg
  • cheddar cheese
  • 1 - bread slice
  • 1 tbs - white vinegar
  • salt
  • fresh cracked pepper

Instructions:

  1. Fill a small pot with water until it is about 4 inches deep.
  2. Add the vinegar to the water.
  3. Crack the egg into a fine sieve. This is to remove the more fluid portions of the egg white, which whisp away during the poaching.
  4. Bring the water to a boil and then lower it to a simmer over medium heat.
  5. Transfer the egg to a small ramekin or tea cup.
  6. Use a slotted spoon to swirl the water mixture in the pot until a uniform vortex is created.
  7. gently slip the egg from the ramekin or tea cup into the center of the vortex.
  8. Start a timer for 3 minutes.
  9. While the egg is cooking, toast the bread and finely slice the cheddar cheese.
  10. Before the egg is done, lightly butter the toast and lay the cheese on top so it melts.
  11. When the timer is up, remove the egg from the pot with a slotted spoon, being careful to drain all the water, and set it on the toast.
  12. Salt and pepper the egg to taste and serve!

    13. Optional:

    • Serve the egg on a toasted burger bun instead of bread toast.
    • Serve with sausage or bacon.
    • Garnish with choppped parsley or spinach.
    • Serve over mashed avocado and a bit of lemon juice.
    • Use fresh mozzarella or some other type of cheese instead of cheddar.
