Breakfast Drinks

Berry Breakfast Smoothie

Amount:

  • 1 Serving

Time:

  • 10 Minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup - frozen mixed berries
  • 1/4 cup - vanilla, honey, or berry yogurt
  • 1/4 cup - oat meal oats
  • 1/4 cup - whole milk

Optional:

  • 1 tbs - honey
  • 1 tsp - sugar
  • 1/4 cup - vanilla ice cream
  • 1/4 cup - almond or oat milk
  • 1/4 cup - fresh berries

Instructions:

  1. Add all the ingredients into a blender.
  2. Blend everything until it's smooth.
  3. Serve!

Irish Coffee

Amount:

  • 1 Serving

Time:

  • 10 Minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz or 4 tbs - Irish whiskey
  • 6 oz or 3/4 cup - dark brewed coffee
  • 1 - sugar cube
  • whipped cream

Instructions:

  1. Pour the hot coffe into an Irish coffee mug.
  2. Drop in the sugar cube.
  3. Gently stir in the whiskey.
  4. Top with whipped cream and serve!

    5. Optional:

    • Substitute 1-2 tsp maple syrup or brown sugar for the standard sugar cube.
    • Garnish with nutmeg, cinnamon, or chocolate shavings.
    • SUbstitute half-and-half for the whipped cream and stir it in.
    • Chill the coffee and serve over an ice cube for a cold and creamy version.

Irish Cream Coffee

Amount:

  • 1 Serving

Time:

  • 10 Minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz or 4 tbs - Irish cream liquer
  • 6 oz or 3/4 cup - dark brewed coffee
  • whipped cream

Instructions:

  1. Pour the hot coffe into an Irish coffee mug.
  2. Stir in the Irish cream.
  3. Top with whipped cream and serve!

    4. Optional:

    • Garnish with nutmeg, cinnamon, or chocolate shavings.
    • Add 1-2 oz of peppermint liquer to give it a holiday twist.
    • Substitute half-and-half for the whipped cream and stir it in.
    • Chill the coffee and serve over an ice cube for a cold and creamy version.
