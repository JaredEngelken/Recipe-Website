Dessert Drinks

Coffee Moon

Amount:

  • 1 Serving

Time:

  • 30 Minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 ounces - lightly spiced rum
  • 1/4 cup - fresh coffee grounds
  • 1 large scoop - vanilla ice cream
  • 1 ounce - heavy cream or whole milk

Instructions:

  1. Place the coffee grounds in a coffee filter over a funnel and measuring cup.
  2. Slowly pour the rum through the coffee grounds.
  3. Empty the measuring cup into another cup and rerun the rum through the coffee a second time.
  4. Add the large scoop of vanilla ice cream to a frosted rocks glass.
  5. Gently pour the coffee infused rum over the ice cream.
  6. Pour the heavy cream or milk over the ice cream to get a nice swirl effect with the rum.
  7. Gently stir the cream and rum together.
  8. Garnish with a bit of shaved chocolate and serve!
  9. The flavor of the cocktail will change as the ice cream melts, making it sweeter, creamier, and full bodied.
