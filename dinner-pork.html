Holiday Ham

Amount:

  • 8+ Servings

Time:

  • 3.5+ Hours

Ingredients:

Ham Ingredients:

  • 1 - ham
  • whole cloves
  • 1 tbs - flour
  • 1 - large oven bag

Glaze Ingredients:

Use half the amount of ingredients for small hams.

  • 1/2 cup - packed dark brown sugar
  • 8 tbs - butter
  • 1/2 cup - dark rum
  • 1/4 cup - molasses
  • 3 tbs - spicy brown mustard
  • 1 tbs - apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp - ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp - ground black pepper

Instructions:

Prepare the Ham and Cook:

  • Pre-heat the oven to 350°F.
  • Shake the flour in the oven bag and set it in a 2 inch deep oven pan.
  • Leaving a thin layer of fat, trim the skin and excess fat from the ham.
  • Lightly score the surface of the ham about 1/4 inch deep in a checkered pattern and poke the cloves into it.
  • Insert the ham into the oven bag.
  • Seal up the oven bag and cut six half-inch slits in the top.
  • Insert a meat thermometer through one of the slits into the thickest part of the ham, not touching the bone.
  • Bake the ham for 2.5 to 3.25 hours or until the thermometer reads 140°F.
  • Let the ham sit in the oven bag for 15 minutes and then serve!

Optional:

  • Make the glaze.
  • Sprinkle the ham in spices like paprika or black pepper for some extra spice a strong browning effect.
  • Add chunks of pineapple to the bag with the ham before baking to give a sweet aroma and flavor.
  • Save the juices for gravy! Seesidesfor a good recipe.

Glaze Instructions:

  1. Mix all the ingredients just prior to use in a medium saucepan and bring to a boil.
  2. Stir constantly while heating.
  3. Reduce heat to a simmer while continuing to stir for 5 to 10 minutes.
  4. Apply to the ham while warm and serve!
