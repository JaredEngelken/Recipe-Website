Breakfast Breads

Streusel Coffee Cake

Amount:

  • 10 Servings

Time:

  • 35 Minutes

Ingredients:

Cinnamon Streusel:

  • 1/3 cup - Bisquick
  • 1/3 cup - packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp - ground cinnamon
  • 2 tbs - firm butter, margarine, or lard

Coffee Cake:

  • 2 cups - Bisquick
  • 2/3 cup - milk or water
  • 2 tbs - sugar
  • 1 - egg

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Grease a 9-inch round pan.
  3. Stir the streusel ingredients together in a small bowl until the mix is crumbly. Then set it aside.
  4. In a medium bowl, mix together the coffee cake ingredients until they're blended.
  5. Pour and spread the batter into the pan.
  6. Sprinkle the streusel on top.
  7. Bake fore 18-22 minutes or until golden brown and then serve!

    8. Variations:

    • Add 3 tbs. more sugar to the batter.
    • Double the amount of streusel topping.
    • Mix half of the streusel into the batter as it's being poured into the pan.
    • Add 1 tsp. of vanilla to the batter.
    • Add 1 tsp. extra cinnamon to teh streusel.
    • Add 1 tsp. of ground nutmeg to the streusel.
    • Add diced fruits such as apples, peaches, prunes, raisins, or blueberries to the batter and topping.
    • Add nuts such as pecans or walnuts to the batter.
    • Drizzle cream cheese glaze frosting on top when serving.

    Notes:

    For high altitude (3,500+ ft.), heat the oven to 425°F, use a square pan, add 2 tbs. of flour to the batter, increase the amount of milk to 3/4 cup, and bake 20+ minutes.
