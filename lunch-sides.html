Chipotle Honey BBQ Sauce

Amount:

  • 2+ Servings

Time:

  • 25 Minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup - ketchup
  • 1/4 cup - honey
  • 1/8 cup - white vinegar
  • 1/8 cup - molasses
  • 1/2 tsp - salt
  • 1/2 tsp - ground black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp - paprika
  • 1/4 tsp - chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp - onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp - garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp - cayenne pepper

Instructions:

  1. Stir the ketchup, honey, vinegar, adn molasses together in a saucepan over medium heat until it's smooth.
  2. Stir in all the dry ingredients.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and simmer the saucefor about 20 minutes until it thickens.

    4. Optional:

    • Add 1 tsp of liquid smoke flavoring.
    • Add more honey to bring out a sweeter honey flavor.
